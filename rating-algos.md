Variants of rating algorithms:

Level 1: Primary Rating
1) It is formed based on data from the user's questionnaire (age, gender, interests, geographical location)
2) Takes into account the completeness of the questionnaire and the number of uploaded photos
3) Includes the user's primary preferences (age range, gender, city)

Level 2: Behavioral rating
1) Dynamically adjusted based on the interaction of other users with
the questionnaire:
1) The number of likes on the questionnaire
2) The ratio of likes and passes
3) Frequency of mutual likes (matches)
4) The frequency of dialog initiation after the match
	a) Includes time parameters (activity at a certain time of day)

Level 3: Combined Rating
1) Integrates primary and behavioral ratings by weight model
2) Takes into account additional factors:  
	c) Inviting friends (referral system)
